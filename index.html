About me

I'm a second-year student at the University of Wyoming pursuing degrees in Statistics and Computer Science with a minor in honors. I'm broadly interested in data-driven approaches to problem-solving and software development. Exploring this interest, I currently work with a research group at the University of Wyoming in the MALLET Lab. My research focuses on the meta-algorithmic technique of automated algorithm selection.
In my free time I enjoy hiking, trail running, skiing and traveling.
