Welcome to my website!

I'm interested in automated machine learning, applications of statistics, and statistical computing. I use this website to exhibit past and current projects.

Currently, I split my time between Eye-to-Eye Telehealth and the Meta-Algorithmics, Learning, and Large-scale Empirical Testing Lab ( MALLET) at the University of Wyoming. In the MALLET lab, I'm exploring automated approaches to identify substrata of data and leveraging these substrata to improve predictive models. Before my current work, I participated in an REU at Carnegie Mellon University working with NBA data on player fatigue. I also interned at Goldman Sachs as a software engineer where I worked on distributed computing software.

Outside of academic pursuits, I enjoy backpacking and trail running. If you are curious about what this looks like in Wyoming and Colorado; in the "Other" section, you'll find a selection of some of my favorite trips.
